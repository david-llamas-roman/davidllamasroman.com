What does 'only' mean in this context?

When it is speaking about GPL software licenses and appear this word, it means that the software is only under the version where appear the before mentioned word. For example, GPL-3.0-only. In this case, this indicates that the software is only under GPL-3.0 version and it cannot be modified or redistributed under previous or future versions.
